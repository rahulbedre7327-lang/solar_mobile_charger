# solar_mobile_charger
## Introduction 
This project is a Solar Mobile Charger developed using a solar panel,battery,charge controller,and boost converter.It converts solar energy into electrical energy to charge mobile phones.
## Objective
-To design an eco-friendly mobile charging system
-To use renewable solar energy
-To provide portable charging during power cuts

## Components Used
-Solar Panel
-TP4056/CN3791 Charge Controller
-18650  Li-ion Battery
-Boost Converter
-USB Port
-Diode
-LED
-Switch

## Working
The solar panel captures sunlight and converts it into electricity.
The charge controller safely charges the battery.
The boost converter increases the voltage  to 5V required for mobile charging through the USB port.

## Advantages
-Uses renewable energy 
-Portable and low cost
-Useful in remote areas
-Reduces electricity consumption

## Applications
- Emergency charging
- Outdoor camping
- Rural areas
- Travel purposes
  ## Conclusion
  The solar mobile chareger is an efficient and environmentally friendly project that helps charge mobile devices using solar energy.
