04 — How It Works
1
Solar Energy Capture
The solar panel (6V–12V) converts sunlight into DC electricity via the photovoltaic effect. Output varies with sunlight intensity.
2
Reverse Protection
The 1N5819 Schottky diode blocks current from flowing back into the panel at night, preventing battery discharge.
3
Charge Controller (MPPT)
The CN3791/TP4056 IC manages charging using CC/CV method. Charges battery to exactly 4.2V, cuts off to prevent overcharging.
4
Energy Storage
The 3.7V Li-Ion 18650 cell stores the harvested solar energy, providing power even at night or during cloudy periods.
5
Voltage Boost to 5V
MT3608/XL6009 boost converter steps up 3.7V battery voltage to a stable regulated 5V output at up to 2A for charging.
6
USB Output to Phone
A standard USB Type-A port delivers 5V/1–2A to any mobile device. LEDs indicate charging status and battery level.
